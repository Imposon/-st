Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 240 ml (8 oz) steamed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of milk foam

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam the milk until it is velvety and creamy.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the cup with espresso, leaving room for the foam.
  4. Top with a spoonful of milk foam.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your latte.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, try adding syrups like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut.
  • To make a **dirty chai latte**, add a shot of chai tea concentrate along with the espresso.
