Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cold brewed coffee
  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar or sweetener (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the cold brewed coffee, ice cubes, and sugar or sweetener.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. If desired, add milk or cream for a richer texture.
  4. Pour the frappe into a glass and top with whipped cream.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your icy, frothy frappe!

Tips

  • For an extra indulgence, drizzle chocolate or caramel syrup on top of the whipped cream.
  • You can use a milk alternative like almond milk or oat milk for a dairy-free option.
