Ingredients

  • 1 cup of hot brewed coffee
  • 1 shot of Irish whiskey
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar
  • Fresh whipped cream

Instructions

  1. Brew a cup of strong coffee.
  2. Add the brown sugar to the hot coffee and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add a shot of Irish whiskey to the coffee and mix well.
  4. Top with a generous dollop of fresh whipped cream.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your Irish Coffee!

Tips

  • Use a good-quality Irish whiskey for the best taste.
  • If you prefer a sweeter version, increase the amount of brown sugar.
