Ingredients

  • 1-2 tablespoons of ground coffee (medium to coarse grind)
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • Milk and sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the ground coffee in a coffee filter or pour-over cone.
  2. Heat water to just below boiling point (around 90°C/194°F).
  3. Slowly pour the hot water over the coffee grounds in a circular motion.
  4. Let the coffee drip through and into the cup.
  5. Serve hot, and add milk and sugar to taste, if desired.

Tips

  • Ensure the coffee grind is not too fine to prevent over-extraction and bitterness.
  • Use freshly ground coffee beans for the best flavor.
