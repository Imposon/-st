Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Hot water (to fill the cup)

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso and pour it into a cup.
  2. Slowly add hot water to the espresso until the desired strength is achieved.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy your Americano!

Tips

  • The strength of your Americano depends on the ratio of espresso to hot water.
  • For a **long black**, reduce the amount of water and pour it over the espresso.
