Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream into a small bowl or glass.
  2. Brew a shot of hot espresso.
  3. Pour the espresso directly over the ice cream.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy your affogato!

Tips

  • Try different flavors of ice cream such as chocolate or hazelnut for a twist.
  • Add a dash of chocolate syrup or caramel on top for extra indulgence.
