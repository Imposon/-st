Ingredients

  • 1 cup of freshly brewed coffee (chilled)
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool down to room temperature or refrigerate to chill.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the chilled coffee over the ice.
  4. Sweeten with sugar if desired and stir well.
  5. Add milk or cream for a creamier taste, and stir again.
  6. Serve and enjoy your refreshing iced coffee!

Tips

  • For an extra kick, try using cold brew coffee instead of regular brewed coffee.
  • You can make coffee ice cubes by freezing brewed coffee to avoid diluting your drink.
