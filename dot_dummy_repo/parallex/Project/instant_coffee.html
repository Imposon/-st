Ingredients

  • 1-2 teaspoons of instant coffee granules
  • 1 cup of hot water or milk
  • Optional: Sugar or milk to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil a cup of water or milk.
  2. Place 1-2 teaspoons of instant coffee granules into a mug.
  3. Pour the hot water or milk into the mug.
  4. Stir to dissolve the coffee granules.
  5. Sweeten with sugar or milk if desired.
  6. Serve immediately.

Tips

  • For a creamier instant coffee, use milk instead of water.
  • Experiment with different flavors by adding cinnamon, vanilla, or flavored syrups.
