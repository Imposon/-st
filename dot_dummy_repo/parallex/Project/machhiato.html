Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • A small amount of steamed milk (1 tablespoon)

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso.
  2. Steam a small amount of milk, just enough to create a velvety foam.
  3. Add a dollop of milk foam on top of the espresso.
  4. Serve immediately in a small cup, typically without any added flavoring.

Tips

  • For a caramel macchiato, drizzle caramel syrup on top of the foam.
  • A macchiato is much stronger than a latte, so enjoy the bold flavor!
