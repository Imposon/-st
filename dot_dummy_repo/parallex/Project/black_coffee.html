Ingredients

  • 1-2 tablespoons ground coffee
  • 1 cup of hot water

Instructions

  1. Boil water and pour over freshly ground coffee.
  2. Stir to mix and allow it to brew for a few minutes.
  3. Serve the coffee black with no sugar or milk.

Tips

  • Use freshly ground coffee for the best flavor.
  • If you want a stronger cup, increase the amount of ground coffee used.
