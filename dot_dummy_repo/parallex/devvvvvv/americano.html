Americano Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 shots of espresso (18-20 grams of coffee)
  • 180-240 ml (6-8 oz) of hot water
  • Optional: Sweetener (sugar, honey) or milk (for a creamier version)

Instructions

  1. Grind the coffee beans finely and brew 1 or 2 shots of espresso (about 30-60 ml, depending on your preference for strength).
  2. Heat 180-240 ml of water to just below boiling (around 90°C or 194°F).
  3. Pour the hot water into your cup first. This helps prevent the espresso from losing flavor when added.
  4. Slowly pour the freshly brewed espresso shots into the hot water.
  5. Stir gently to combine the espresso with the hot water.
  6. Optional: Add sweetener or a splash of milk if desired, and stir again.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your Americano!

Tips

  • For a stronger Americano, use 2 shots of espresso, or even 3 if you prefer a more intense flavor.
  • Make sure the water is hot but not boiling, as water that is too hot can burn the espresso, leading to a bitter taste.
