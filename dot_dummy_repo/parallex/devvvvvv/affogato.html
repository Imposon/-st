Ingredients

  • 1 shot of hot espresso (about 30 ml)
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato
  • Optional: Chocolate shavings, cocoa powder, or crushed nuts for garnish
  • Optional: Liqueur such as amaretto or Kahlúa for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Start by brewing a shot of espresso using your espresso machine or a stovetop espresso maker.
  2. Place one scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato in a small glass or cup.
  3. Once the espresso is ready, pour it directly over the scoop of ice cream. The hot espresso will melt the ice cream slightly, creating a rich, creamy mixture.
  4. Optional: Garnish with chocolate shavings, cocoa powder, or crushed nuts for an added touch of flavor and texture.
  5. If you like, add a splash of liqueur such as amaretto or Kahlúa for an extra indulgent twist.
  6. Serve immediately while the ice cream is still cold and the espresso is hot.

Tips

  • For the best flavor, use high-quality espresso and rich vanilla ice cream or gelato. The combination of the hot and cold elements is key to this dessert's unique appeal.
  • If you prefer a less sweet version, you can use a less sweet or unsweetened vanilla ice cream.
  • Affogato can also be made with other ice cream flavors like chocolate or hazelnut, but vanilla is the traditional choice.

Pro Tip: To enhance the experience, serve your affogato in a clear glass to show off the contrast between the hot espresso and the melting ice cream.
