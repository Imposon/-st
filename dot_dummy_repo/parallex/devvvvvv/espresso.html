Espresso Recipe

Ingredients

  • 18-20 grams of espresso coffee beans
  • Water (filtered if possible)

Instructions

  1. Grind the espresso beans to a fine consistency (similar to table salt).
  2. Preheat your espresso machine and portafilter to around 90-96°C (194-205°F).
  3. Tamp the ground coffee evenly into the portafilter.
  4. Lock the portafilter into the espresso machine and start brewing.
  5. Allow the espresso to brew for about 25-30 seconds (or until a rich, dark shot is extracted).
  6. Serve immediately in a pre-warmed cup.

Tips

  • Make sure your espresso machine is clean and free of old coffee residue for the best taste.
  • Use freshly ground coffee beans for optimal flavor.
  • Experiment with grind size, tamping pressure, and brew time for the perfect shot!
  • Ensure your water temperature is between 90°C and 96°C (194°F and 205°F) for the best extraction.
