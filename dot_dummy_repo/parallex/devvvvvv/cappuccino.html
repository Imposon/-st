Cappuccino Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (18-20 grams of coffee)
  • 120 ml (about 4 oz) of fresh whole milk
  • Optional: Sweetener (sugar, cinnamon, or flavored syrups)

Instructions

  1. Grind the coffee beans finely and brew a shot of espresso (about 30 ml).
  2. Steam the milk using a steam wand until it reaches 65-70째C (149-158째F), creating a velvety texture with a thick foam.
  3. Pour the shot of espresso into a warm cup.
  4. Slowly pour the steamed milk into the espresso, aiming for a ratio of about 1:1:1 (one part espresso, one part steamed milk, one part foam).
  5. Spoon the foam on top to form a thick, frothy cap over the espresso.
  6. Optional: Add sweetener (sugar, cinnamon, or syrups) to taste, and stir gently.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips

  • For the perfect foam, use whole milk or a high-fat milk alternative, like oat milk.
  • Ensure your milk is steamed to the right temperature (65-70째C or 149-158째F). Too hot, and it can taste burnt; too cold, and it won't foam well.
  • If you want to make a stronger cappuccino, you can add a double shot of espresso.
  • Experiment with adding a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top of the foam for added flavor.

Pro Tip: To create a beautiful cappuccino with perfect foam, practice pouring the milk slowly into the espresso and finish with a spoonful of foam on top. The foam should be thick enough to hold its shape.
