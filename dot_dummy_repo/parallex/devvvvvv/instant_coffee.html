Instant Coffee Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1-2 teaspoons of instant coffee (depending on desired strength)
  • 1 cup (240 ml) of hot water
  • Optional: Milk or cream (to taste)
  • Optional: Sugar or sweetener (to taste)
  • Optional: Flavored syrups or spices (e.g., vanilla, cinnamon) for added flavor

Instructions

  1. Boil water. For the best taste, use fresh water heated to just below boiling point (around 200°F or 93°C).
  2. Place 1-2 teaspoons of instant coffee into your coffee mug, depending on how strong you prefer your coffee.
  3. Once the water is ready, pour it into the mug over the instant coffee. Stir well until the coffee granules dissolve completely.
  4. If desired, add sugar or sweetener to taste and stir until fully dissolved. You can also add flavored syrups or spices like cinnamon or vanilla for an extra flavor boost.
  5. For creamier coffee, add milk or cream to your taste and stir to combine. You can use any type of milk or milk alternative (e.g., oat milk, almond milk).
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your quick and easy instant coffee!

Tips

  • Use freshly boiled water, as it helps dissolve the coffee more effectively and creates a smoother taste.
  • If you prefer your coffee stronger, you can adjust the amount of instant coffee to suit your taste.
  • For an iced version, prepare the coffee as usual, then let it cool down or pour it over ice for a refreshing treat.
  • Instant coffee can also be used as an ingredient in baking, smoothies, or even in your favorite coffee-flavored desserts.

Pro Tip: For a frothy texture, add a little milk or cream to your mug and froth it using a milk frother before stirring in the coffee. This adds a velvety texture to your instant coffee!
