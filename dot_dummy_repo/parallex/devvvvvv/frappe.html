Frappe Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1-2 teaspoons of instant coffee (adjust based on your preferred strength)
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar (optional, to taste)
  • 50-60 ml of cold water (about 1/4 cup)
  • Ice cubes
  • Cold milk or milk alternative (to taste)
  • Optional: whipped cream for topping

Instructions

  1. In a shaker or a tall glass, combine the instant coffee, sugar (if using), and cold water.
  2. Using a milk frother, handheld whisk, or a blender, vigorously mix the coffee and water for about 20-30 seconds, until it becomes frothy and a thick foam forms on top.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  4. Pour the frothy coffee mixture over the ice cubes.
  5. Top with cold milk or milk alternative (e.g., oat milk, almond milk), to your taste. Stir gently to combine.
  6. Optional: Add a dollop of whipped cream for an extra indulgent treat!
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing frappe!

Tips

  • If you like your frappe sweeter, you can adjust the amount of sugar or add flavored syrups like vanilla or caramel.
  • For an extra creamy frappe, use full-fat milk or a creamy milk alternative like coconut milk.
  • If you prefer a stronger coffee flavor, use more instant coffee or add a shot of espresso.
  • To make your frappe even colder, use frozen coffee cubes instead of regular ice cubes.
