Coffee Guide

Your ultimate guide to the world of coffee

Types of Coffee

  • Espresso: A full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee served in "shots".
  • Latte: A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.
  • Cappuccino: An espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, prepared with steamed milk foam.
  • Americano: A style of coffee prepared by brewing espresso with added hot water.
  • Macchiato: An espresso with a small amount of frothy milk.

Brewing Methods

  • French Press: A method of manually brewing coffee by steeping coarse-ground coffee beans in hot water and then pressing them through a mesh filter.
  • Pour Over: A method of brewing coffee by pouring hot water over coffee grounds in a filter.
  • Aeropress: A device for brewing coffee that involves steeping coffee grounds and then forcing them through a filter by pressing a plunger through a tube.
  • Drip Coffee Maker: A common household coffee brewing device that automatically drips hot water through coffee grounds and a filter.
  • Cold Brew: Coffee brewed with cold water over a long period, usually 12 hours or more.
