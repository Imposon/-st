Dalgona Coffee Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 cup (240 ml) of cold milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for iced version)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water.
  2. Using a hand whisk, hand mixer, or stand mixer, whisk the mixture vigorously for 2-3 minutes until it becomes thick and frothy. The consistency should be light and creamy.
  3. Fill a glass with cold milk. If you like, you can add ice cubes for a refreshing iced version.
  4. Spoon the whipped coffee mixture on top of the milk. The frothy coffee should float on top of the milk.
  5. Stir the whipped coffee into the milk before drinking, or enjoy it as is with the frothy top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your creamy, frothy Dalgona coffee!

  • For a sweeter Dalgona coffee, add more sugar, or experiment with flavored syrups (e.g., vanilla, caramel).
  • If you want a stronger coffee flavor, you can increase the amount of instant coffee in the whipped mixture.

Pro Tip: For an extra creamy texture, use full-fat milk or a rich milk alternative like coconut milk, which will make the drink more velvety.
