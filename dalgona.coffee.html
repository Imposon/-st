Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 cup of milk (or milk alternative)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine instant coffee, sugar, and hot water.
  2. Whisk the mixture until it becomes thick and frothy (about 3-5 minutes with an electric mixer).
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the milk over the ice.
  4. Top the milk with the whipped coffee mixture.
  5. Serve and enjoy your fluffy Dalgona coffee!

Tips

  • Use an electric mixer or whisk vigorously to get the frothy texture.
  • Experiment with adding flavorings like vanilla or chocolate to the whipped coffee.
