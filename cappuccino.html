Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 1/2 cup steamed milk
  • 1/2 cup milk foam

Instructions

  1. Brew a shot of espresso and pour it into a cup.
  2. Steam the milk until it has a velvety texture.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the cup with espresso, leaving space for the foam.
  4. Top with a thick layer of milk foam.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your cappuccino!

Tips

  • Use a milk frother to create the perfect milk foam.
  • Sprinkle a bit of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top for extra flavor.
