Ingredients

  • 18 grams of finely ground espresso coffee
  • 60 ml of hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the espresso machine and portafilter.
  2. Place the finely ground coffee into the portafilter and tamp it down evenly.
  3. Lock the portafilter into the espresso machine.
  4. Start the espresso shot and extract 60 ml of espresso within 25-30 seconds.
  5. Serve the espresso in a small cup.

Tips

  • Adjust the grind size for a better extraction if the shot is too bitter or sour.
  • For a **double espresso**, simply use 36 grams of coffee and extract 120 ml.
