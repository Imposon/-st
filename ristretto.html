Ingredients

  • 18-20 grams of finely ground coffee
  • 30 ml (1 oz) of hot water

Instructions

  1. Grind the coffee to a fine texture.
  2. Use a ristretto shot setting on your espresso machine.
  3. Pour 30 ml of hot water over the coffee grounds.
  4. Extract for around 15-20 seconds to get a concentrated, intense shot.
  5. Serve immediately in a small cup.

Tips

  • Ristretto uses less water than a regular espresso for a stronger, more concentrated flavor.
  • Adjust the grind and amount of coffee for the perfect shot.
